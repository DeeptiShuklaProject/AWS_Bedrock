

# Writer

The following Writer models are available in Amazon Bedrock:


| **Model** | **Description** | 
| --- | --- | 
| [Palmyra Vision 7B](model-card-writer-palmyra-vision-7b.md) | Palmyra Vision 7B is Writer's advanced multimodal language model for visual analysis, excelling at image understanding, handwritten text extraction, and chart interpretation. | 
| [Palmyra X5](model-card-writer-palmyra-x5.md) | Palmyra X5 is Writer's enterprise model with improved reasoning, coding, and agentic capabilities for complex business workflows. | 
| [Palmyra X4](model-card-writer-palmyra-x4.md) | Palmyra X4 is Writer's enterprise LLM optimized for business writing, content generation, and knowledge work with strong instruction following. |
