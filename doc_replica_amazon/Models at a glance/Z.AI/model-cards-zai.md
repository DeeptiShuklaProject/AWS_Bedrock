

# Z.AI
<a name="model-cards-zai"></a>

The following Z.AI models are available in Amazon Bedrock:


| **Model** | **Description** | 
| --- | --- | 
| [GLM 4.7](model-card-zai-glm-4-7.md) | GLM 4.7 is Z.AI's large language model with strong multilingual capabilities and solid performance on reasoning, coding, and knowledge benchmarks. | 
| [GLM 4.7 Flash](model-card-zai-glm-4-7-flash.md) | GLM 4.7 Flash is Z.AI's lightweight model optimized for fast inference and low-latency tasks while maintaining strong general capabilities. | 
| [GLM 5](model-card-zai-glm-5.md) | GLM 5 is Z.AI's frontier-class general-purpose model with 744B parameters and 40B active, optimized for complex systems engineering and long-horizon agentic tasks. |
